        How to play Brawl Stars?

        Enjoy exciting and frenetic 3-on-3 multiplayer battles in Brawl Stars! Prepare to survive as you face waves of enemies ready to take your life in an intense 3-minute battle.

        Unlock all kinds of super attacks and improve your skills to beat your opponents! Navigate through dozens of mysterious locations full of obstacles and barriers to find the fastest way to finish off your opponent's team with almost no damage. Fight in a variety of mysterious locations, collecting enough stars with each victory to prevent others from taking them away from you! Good luck...
